Instructions to Get Started

Follow these steps to securely grant access and activate our partnership.

Pre-start Security Checklist

  • Ensure you are an administrator of the Meta Business Account to which you are granting access.
  • Verify that two-factor authentication (2FA) is enabled on your personal Facebook account. This is critical for protection.
  • It is recommended to have at least two administrators in your business account for added security.

Why We Use Partner Access

We request access as a "Partner," not as an "Individual," because it is the industry security standard. This method is not tied to our employees' personal accounts but is granted to our agency as an organization. This centralizes management, enhances security, and protects your assets from unauthorized access.

Follow These Steps

1

Go to Business Settings

Open your Meta Business Suite settings by clicking this link: business.facebook.com/settings. Select the correct business account from the dropdown menu on the left.

2

Find the "Partners" Section

In the left menu, under "Users," select "Partners." Then, click the blue "Add" button and choose "Give a partner access to your assets."

3

Enter our Business Account ID

In the window that appears, paste our agency's Business Account ID. Click the button below to copy it.

1461249777976011

After pasting the ID, click "Next."

4

Select Assets and Assign Access

On this step, choose which assets you are granting access to. We will need the following permissions for full functionality:

1. In the left column, select "Pages." Choose your Facebook Page and, in the right column, activate the "Full control" -> "Manage" toggle.

2. In the left column, select "Ad accounts." Choose your ad account and activate the "Full control" -> "Manage" toggle.

3. In the left column, select "Instagram accounts." Choose your Instagram profile and activate the "Full control" -> "Manage" toggle.

4. In the left column, select "Pixels." Choose your pixel and activate the "Full control" -> "Manage" toggle.

✓

Done!

Click the "Save Changes" button. We will receive a notification and, after accepting the invitation, we can begin our work. Thank you for your trust!

Your Security is Our Priority

We understand how important the security of your business assets is. That's why we exclusively use the official and most secure access method recommended by Meta.

✓ No Passwords Exchanged

You will never share your personal Facebook or Instagram logins or passwords with us. The entire process occurs within Meta's secure system.

✓ Access for an Organization, Not an Individual

You grant access to our agency as a "Partner," not to an employee's personal account. This means access is centrally managed and not dependent on specific individuals, eliminating risks during personnel changes.

✓ You Retain Full Control

You are the sole owner of your assets. At any time, you can review, modify, or completely revoke the access you've granted us in your Business Suite settings.

✓ Protection Against Fraud

This method protects you from phishing and attempts by scammers to gain access to your data, as it does not require clicking on suspicious links or sharing confidential information with third parties.

By entrusting us with access, you can be confident that we adhere to the highest security standards to protect your business.
